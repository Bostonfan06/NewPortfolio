ABOUT ME

Passionate and hard-working web developer, graphic designer with a flair for creating elegant websites, applications and graphics.

Hi! My name is Sean McPheeters. I was born and raised in Santa Clarita, CA. Ever since an early age, I've had an affinity for art and computers. It wasn't until I got older, that I realized I could combine the two. Nothing compares to the feeling of having a blank page, and being able to turn it into a full functioning project. When I'm not "wired in", you will find me hiking, drawing, playing fetch with my dogs, or snowboarding.
